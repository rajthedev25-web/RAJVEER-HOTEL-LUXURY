Our Commitment

At Grand Essence, we understand that true luxury is inseparable from privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your personal data with the same care and attention we provide during your stay.

1. Information Collection

To provide a bespoke experience, we collect essential details such as your preferences, dietary requirements, and stay history. This allows us to anticipate your needs before you even arrive.

2. Uncompromising Security

All digital records are encrypted using military-grade protocols. Our physical servers are housed in secure, monitored facilities to ensure your itinerary remains strictly confidential.

3. Third-Party Policy

Grand Essence does not sell, trade, or share your personal information with external marketers. We only share data with trusted partners (e.g., private jet charters) strictly for fulfilling your requested services.

4. Your Rights

You have the absolute right to request a full copy of your data or ask for its permanent deletion from our royal archives at any time.
